MagicPhotos 1.14.0

Developer: Oleg Derevenetz <oleg.derevenetz@gmail.com>

MagicPhotos is an application for Windows Phone devices that lets you quickly and easily give photos a dramatic look by applying different effects while keeping your chosen details untouched.

If you have any questions regarding this application, you can contact application developer on this website: http://magicphotos.sourceforge.net/.

Privacy policy for this application is located here: http://magicphotos.sourceforge.net/privacy.php.

Decolorize mode

Decolorize mode lets you give photos a dramatic look by converting them to black and white, while keeping chosen details in color. This effect draws the viewer's attention to the colored areas, creating striking images. Use the following buttons in this mode:

button to move and resize image

button to bring back original color of the part of an image

button to make part of an image black and white

Sketch mode

Sketch mode lets you convert your photos to sketches, while keeping chosen details untouched. You will have an opportunity to control sketch generation process. Use the following buttons in this mode:

button to move and resize image

button to bring back original look of the part of an image

button to convert part of an image to sketch

Cartoon mode

Cartoon mode lets you convert your photos to cartoons (color sketches), while keeping chosen details untouched. You will have an opportunity to control cartoon generation process. Use the following buttons in this mode:

button to move and resize image

button to bring back original look of the part of an image

button to convert part of an image to cartoon

Blur mode

Blur mode lets you to apply the blur effect to chosen details of your image. You will have an opportunity to control blur process. Use the following buttons in this mode:

button to move and resize image

button to bring back original look of the part of an image

button to apply blur to the part of an image

Pixelate mode

Pixelate mode lets you to apply pixelate effect to chosen details of your image. You will have an opportunity to control pixelization process. Use the following buttons in this mode:

button to move and resize image

button to bring back original look of the part of an image

button to apply pixelate to the part of an image

Recolor mode

Recolor mode lets you recolor your photos to create amazing new artwork and effects. Use the following buttons in this mode:

button to move and resize image

button to bring back original color of the part of an image

button to apply selected color to the part of an image

button to select a desired color from the palette

Retouch mode

Retouch mode lets you remove unwanted content from your photos using Clone Stamp and Blur techniques. Here are some tips for removing unwanted content:

1. Open photo from which you want to remove objects.

2. Click on the button and then click somewhere on your photo to pick a good sample. Wherever you take a sample, that's what your going to be drawing in place of the removed object.

3. Click on the button and then carefully press and drag your finger over the object you want to remove. You will notice that the spot where you took the sample will follow your movements.

4. Continue to repeat steps 2 and 3 until the unwanted object is completely gone. You might have to change techniques a little based on available places to take a good sample. Use zoom and helper screen for accurate positioning if needed. Use button to blur sharp boundaries if needed.

5. Save changed image to photo gallery on your device.
